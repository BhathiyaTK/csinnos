ARE YOU LOOKING FOR A SUSTAINABLE PACKAGING SOLUTION?

Our mycelium packaging solution, HAIFI is the answer that you have been looking for as it is proven to be the ultimate green material for the future.

WHAT IS MYCELIUM PACKAGING?

Mycelium packaging is a 100% BIODEGRADABLE, high-performing packaging solution bioengineered using root-like structures of fungi named mycelium as binding agents and agricultural waste.

WHY GO WITH THIS OPTION?

  • 100% natural and non-toxic
  • High shock resistance
  • Mouldable into an endless variety of custom shapes
  • Lightweight
